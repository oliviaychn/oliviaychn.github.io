About Me

Hello! My name is Olivia Chen, and this website is a small introduction to who I am and what I care about. I am a student who is deeply interested in finance, data, and the ways that analytical thinking can be used to solve real-world problems. Over time, I have become especially interested in topics like investing, business strategy, entrepreneurship, and the intersection between quantitative skills and decision-making. What excites me most is that these fields are never isolated from each other. Finance depends on understanding people, data science depends on asking the right questions, and entrepreneurship depends on turning ideas into something meaningful and practical.

Outside of academics, I also enjoy exploring creative interests. I like thinking about how design, storytelling, and presentation shape the way ideas are communicated. Whether I am working on a school project, learning a new technical skill, or reflecting on a personal experience, I always try to connect structure with creativity. That balance is something I value a lot. I believe that strong ideas become even more powerful when they are expressed clearly and thoughtfully.

This website highlights some of the interests that motivate me. On the next page, I talk more about the topics I am passionate about and the kinds of activities I enjoy spending time on. I wanted this site to feel simple, personal, and clear, while also showing my ability to create a basic multi-page website using HTML and CSS. Thank you for visiting, and I hope this gives you a better sense of who I am.
